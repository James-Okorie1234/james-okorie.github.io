## About Me

I'm an MPhil Physics candidate specializing in Embedded Systems and Automation, currently developing a real-time thermal camera system for Fall Armyworm detection in maize farms. I'm seeking a PhD position to advance research at the intersection of IoT, robotics, and AI — contributing to smart farming technologies and climate-smart solutions.

My research interests include:
- Integrating IoT, robotics, and automation to enhance smart farming technologies
- Developing sensor-based agricultural systems for crop monitoring, with a focus on real-time pest and disease detection using imaging techniques
- Applying AI/ML for improved yield prediction and pest detection to drive innovation in automated farming
